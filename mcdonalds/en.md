title: McDonalds
brands: McDonald's, McCafé, McDelivery
crime: Largest fast food plastic waste generator — straws, cups, wrappers, packaging
region: Entire planet, urban environments, oceans
---
McDonald's is one of the largest sources of fast food plastic litter in the world. The corporation serves over 69 million customers daily, generating billions of plastic straws, cups, wrappers and bags. Its packaging is routinely found among the most common items in coastal and urban cleanups.
