---
title: Starbucks
brands: Starbucks Coffee, Frappuccino, Teavana, Evolution Fresh
crime: Billions of disposable cups with plastic lids — largely unrecyclable
region: Entire planet, urban waste streams
---
Starbucks produces approximately 8 billion disposable cups per year. Despite pledges to introduce reusable cups, the vast majority of cups used are single-use, lined with plastic, and end up in landfills rather than recycling streams.
